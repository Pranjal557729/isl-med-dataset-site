ISL_MED Dataset

Indian Sign Language Medical Gesture Dataset

Overview

The ISL_MED dataset contains 6,726 gesture videos covering 49 distinct medical terms in Indian Sign Language (ISL). It was collected from 15 native ISL signers at the Deaf & Dumb School in Jorhat, Assam, India.

This dataset supports research in gesture recognition for healthcare access and assistive technology.
